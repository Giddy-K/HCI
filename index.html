Who We Are

Evidence-Based Design for Real Impact

Redline UX Collective is a multidisciplinary design team formed at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology dedicated to solving complex user experience challenges in government and public sector systems.

Our mission is to make essential digital services accessible, efficient, and user-friendly for all citizens, regardless of their technical expertise.

We combine expertise in user research, interaction design, accessibility, and evaluation to create evidence-based design solutions that measurably improve user outcomes.

Our approach is grounded in human-centered design principles, rigorous usability testing, and iterative refinement based on real user feedback.
