Project Overview

The County E-Services Platform project involved creating a unified digital platform for Nairobi County Government services. The platform consolidates multiple citizen services into a single, accessible interface.

The Challenge

Citizens had to visit multiple offices and websites to access different county services, leading to confusion, long wait times, and inefficient service delivery.

Our Solution

A unified platform that integrates business permits, land rates payments, parking services, health certificates, and general inquiries into one user-friendly interface.

Key Features

  • Single sign-on for all services
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • M-PESA payment integration
  • Real-time application tracking
  • Multi-language support (English & Swahili)

Results

  • 5 services successfully integrated
  • 92% user satisfaction rating
  • 60% reduction in processing time
  • 45% increase in online transactions
